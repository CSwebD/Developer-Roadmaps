What is DevOps Engineering?


Description


DevOps engineering is a software development approach that combines development (Dev) and operations (Ops) teams to improve collaboration, efficiency, and the overall software development lifecycle. DevOps engineers are responsible for bridging the gap between development and operations, focusing on automation, continuous integration, continuous delivery, and infrastructure management. They work on creating and maintaining the systems and tools needed for efficient software development, deployment, monitoring, and scaling. DevOps engineers strive for a culture of shared responsibility, fast feedback loops, and iterative improvements. Their goal is to streamline processes, enhance software quality, and ensure smooth and reliable delivery of applications to end users.
